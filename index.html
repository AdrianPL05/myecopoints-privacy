Privacy Policy for MyEcoPoints

Effective Date: 11 March 2026

1. Introduction

MyEcoPoints is an early-stage beta application designed to scan barcodes on grocery products and display information about ecological certifications. This Privacy Policy outlines what personal data we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and your rights as a user.

2. Data We Collect

To provide core functionality, MyEcoPoints collects the following information:

  • Email Address: used to create an account and log in; linked to the user's identity.
  • Name, Phone Number, Password: collected during registration; may be fictitious for testing purposes.

No other personal or sensitive information is collected in this beta version. In future updates, additional information may be collected only with user consent.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide account functionality, login, and access to the app.
  • To display product information based on scanned barcodes.
  • To communicate with users regarding support or updates.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Currently, we do not share any personal data with third parties. Data is stored securely and used solely for app functionality. In future versions, if we partner with product companies for cashback, data sharing will occur only with explicit user consent.

5. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Data is encrypted in transit and stored securely.
