MyEcoPoints

Privacy Policy

MyEcoPoints is a beta application for scanning barcodes on grocery products and viewing ecological product information. The app collects minimal personal data only for app functionality, including:

  • Email address (used for logging in and identifying user account)
  • Name, phone number, password (used for registration; may be fictitious for testing purposes)

No other personal or sensitive information is collected. All data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties.

Support

For any questions or issues while testing MyEcoPoints, please contact our support team at:

Email: support@myecopoints.com

Notes

This app is an early-stage beta. Features like cashback and payouts are not yet active and will be added in future updates.
